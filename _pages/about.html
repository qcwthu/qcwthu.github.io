Hi! I'm Chengwei Qin. I graduated from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. Now I am a final-year PhD candidate in School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU). I work in Natural Language Processing, especially large language models (LLMs), under Prof. Shafiq Joty. My research interest primarily lies in enabling LLMs to effectively and efficiently adapt to new tasks while retaining prior knowledge as well as exploring the reasoning capability of LLMs.
